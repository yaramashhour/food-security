Vertical Farming Techniques

Vertical farming is a method of growing crops in vertically stacked layers, often in controlled indoor environments, using techniques like hydroponics or aeroponics with artificial lighting instead of soil and sunlight. This space-efficient approach allows for higher yields per square foot, year-round production, and reduced land and water use, making it a technology for more sustainable food production, especially in urban areas.

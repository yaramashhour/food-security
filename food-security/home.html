Overview

Food security is a critical challenge in Jordan, shaped by limited water resources, climate change, and population growth. Agriculture plays a vital role in ensuring national food supply, creating jobs, and supporting rural livelihoods. Despite having only a small share of arable land, Jordan has developed innovative farming practices to adapt to its harsh environment.
